About Department

    The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) was founded in 2008, providing an undergraduate program in Electronics and Communication Engineering. Initially, the program had an intake of 60 students, which steadily rose to 198 by 2023. In pursuit of its mission and vision, the department boasts ample infrastructure, including well-equipped laboratories and licensed software such as Xilinx, Code Composer Studio, MATLAB, LABTEK, Microwind, NS-2 to develop intelligent systems and MASM, ensuring comprehensive support for academic endeavors. The Department is committed to providing students with access to cutting-edge technologies through various avenues such as NPTEL online resources, webinars conducted by Texas Instruments, spoken tutorials, SWAYAM, and FOSSEE by IIT Bombay. Additionally, students benefit from hands-on experience with LabVIEW through collaboration with National Instruments (NI), as well as workshops and internships organized in partnership with the Andhra Pradesh State Skill Development Corporation (APSSDC).
