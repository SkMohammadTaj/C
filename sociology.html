About Department

    The Sociology course at our college delves into the study of human societies, social behaviors, and cultural norms. Students examine social structures, institutions, and the dynamics of social change. The program emphasizes research methods and critical thinking, preparing students to analyze complex social issues. With a focus on contemporary and historical perspectives, the course equips graduates for careers in social work, public policy, and community development. Our faculty bring real-world experience to the classroom, fostering a deeper understanding of societal interactions.
