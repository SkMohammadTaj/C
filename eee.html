About Department

    The Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department, established in the year 2008, has been well structured. The department is running with well qualified, dedicated and experienced faculty. All faculty are with minimum M.Tech qualification to fulfill the aspirations of student community. It is offering B.Tech. (EEE) programme with an annual intake of 66 and providing outstanding facilities for all the students to learn innovative technologies. The department has exclusive departmental library with large number of titles and volumes. The library has Intranet/Internet facilities to access AUTOLIB , OPAC , NPTEL and online journals. As a part of the curriculum, Industrial visits are arranged for students to upgrade their skill and knowledge. Our EEE students also actively participated in paper presentation contest organized at various Engineering Colleges and Universities. Some of them have been awarded in these Paper presentation contests. The department is having full-fledged laboratories to meet the requirements of curriculum. The department is also having projects and R&D lab with 10 systems running with updated software.
